Labyrinth

Labyrinth is a film directed by Jim Henson in 1986. It is considered a musical fantasy film. It follows a young girl, Sarah, as she journeys through a maze to save her baby brother from the Goblin King.

Characters

  • Jaraeth, King of Goblins (David Bowie)
  • Sarah Williams (Jennifer Connelly)
  • Toby Froud (Toby Williams)
  • Shelley Thomspon (Irene Williams)
  • Christopher Malcom (Robert William)

Movie Details

This story is about a young girl's struggles with taking care of a younger sibbling and how saying things sometimes can not be taken back. In a fit of anger she wished her brother would be taken away by the Goblin King from her favorite book. Then her brother is taken by goblins and she has 13 hours to save her brother from a labyrinth or he will turn into a goblin forever.
